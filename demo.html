Americhem Sales Corporation

Americhem Sales Corporation (ACS) distributes chemicals in the Midwest and the United States. It offers automotive products, including motor oils, heavy duty gear oils, pre-mixed washer solvents, antifreeze products, and high octane racing fuels; metal working fluids, such as cutting oils, drawing compounds, grinding fluids, and soluble oils; specialty products, which include base oils, mineral seal oils, and specialty oils; and caustic soda, sulfuric acid, caustic potash, and hydrogen peroxide. The company also offers olefins, polyolefins, aromatics, alpha olefins, styrenics, specialty chemicals, plastic piping, and polymer resins. In addition, it provides lab testing and custom blending/packaging services.

Americhem Sales Corporation was founded in 1970 and is based in Mason, Michigan. ACS sells its products through gas stations and dealers. The company was formerly known as Americhem Corporation and changed its name to Americhem Sales Corporation in 1998.
